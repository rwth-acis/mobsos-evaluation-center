Welcome to the MobSOS Evaluation Center

MobSOS Evaluation Center is a web application that allows you to evaluate your organization's services' success by analyzing their performance over time.

Features

Analyze

Get insights into how your services are used by the community.

Test

Test SQL queries and analyze log messages in order to find potential bugs in your services.

Awareness

Increase success awareness in the community by providing success models which are easy to understand.

Create your first community and go to success modeling to create your first success model.
